# E-commerce Sales Analysis Report

## Objective
To analyze sales data and identify trends using data visualization techniques.

## Key Findings
- Electronics category generates the highest revenue.
- Monthly sales show noticeable seasonal variation.
- Revenue distribution highlights dependency on top categories.

## Insights
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories.
- Plan inventory based on monthly trends.
- Diversify product offerings to reduce revenue concentration risk.

## Conclusion
Data visualization provides quick and effective understanding of sales performance and business trends.
